Something About Me

Hey, there! I am Fangliang from Hong Kong. Actually, I was born in Shaoxing, Zhejiang Province, Mainland China.

I received my bacholar degree in Accounting from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2011.

Worked at KPMG Hong Kong for two years then, I realized those routines I was performing as an professional accountant can be programmed and efficiently done by computers. Clearly, more and more sophisticated tasks can be executed by smart machines with the advancement of technology. It seems the very thing that a person need to know in this century is how to program, i.e. know how to talk to machines. That is why I came to BU as a LEAP student and set off my new journey here.
